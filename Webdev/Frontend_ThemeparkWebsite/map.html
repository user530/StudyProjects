Docking Station - "Argonaut"

"Any spacecraft passing through the wormhole must first enter the docking station..."

Entrance to the theme park consists of parking lot, tickets booths and transfers. For adults and children of all ages.


Paradise World - Achenar 7

"Achenar 7 is a shining jewel of the Achenar system, denoted as "Paradise world". It boasts a mild climate and one of the most exotic flora and fauna. The beaches of the northern continent are a wonder waiting to be discovered with its very fine white sand and a sea with infinite nuances of turquoise..."

Resort area, waterpark, swimming pools, spa, beach houses. For adults and children of all ages.


Colony's Capital - Peters City

"The capital of the human mission in the Aachenar system bears the name of the leader of the pioneers from Earth. Located on the southern continent of this planet, Peters City has a very developed infrastructure, centered on the spaceport... In terms of magnificence, this city is on a par with the capitals of the old Earth and is a favorite destination for interplanetary tourists..."

Hotels, restaurants, plaza, venue spots. For adults and children of all ages.


Central Marketplace

"The heart of the economy of the sector - the central market of the Planet's capital is a cacophony of sounds, colors, and smells. Here you can find everything you need and even much that you did not know you need. Besides, this market is full of various catering facilities and is a gourmet paradise. Any traveler must visit this market at least once..."

Gift shops, booths, food corts, cafeteria, etc. For adults and children of all ages.


Outer ring - Recreation area

"The outer rim belting the space station is the recreation area, consisting mostly of parks and lounges. This area is always filled with vacationers, regardless of the time of day. Four main sectors of this zone are dedicated to the main classes that keep the station going: workers, scientists, soldiers, and craftsmen..."

Parks, lounges, picnic areas, concert halls and agoras. For adults and children of all ages.


Stellar academy - Yamaha's glory

"All pilots of the Achenar system - from famous captains to the most humble second officer - start from this very Academy, named after the famous hero of the Troy fleet. The first step in the past to glory is the simple simulators that allow young recruits to get used to the harsh realities of the space..."

Flat rides, Tilt-a-whirls, Scramblers, Spining Ufo's, Swing rides, and many many others. Rides oriented on young children, toddlers and their parents, and also other families friendly entertainment.


Academy Canteen

"All the corridors of the academy lead to the canteen, as old saying goes. Here you will be able to satisfy your hunger and rest before or after hardships of learning process..."

Food and drinks, music and other forms of entertainment. For adults and children of all ages.


Academy Holodeck

"When the recruit shows exceptional skills, his superiors allow him to test in near-real conditions on the Holodeck. This stage offers the most challenging and grueling challenges that assess abilities required from the officer of the Achenar's fleet..."

The VR-zone offers a multitude of different games. Recommended for teenagers and adults.


Academy Simulation Chambers

"After the recruit completed a crash course in the Academy classes, he starts practicing using computer simulations. They reproduce the difficult conditions in which real pilots operate to ensure that the recruit is fully prepared for his duty...."

The arcade consists of over 200 videogames of different genres to satisfy any customer. Recommended for children of all ages and adults.


Academy Proving Ground

"Before the graduation ceremony, all recruits are must complete the final test on proving grounds. This is a real-life exercise in extreme conditions on the hostile planet of Achenar 8. Only 7% of attendees complete the full course, and incidents occur very frequently, but nevertheless, every year hundreds of brave men and women dare to test themselves here..."

Are you prepared for the most adrenaline-inducing coasters on the planet? Wormhole's Proving Grounds will offer you an unmatched thrill and surge of endorphins. Adults only.


Kagwa Station - Militia Training

"Space is full of mysteries and secrets waiting to be untold, but sometimes, to peek into the unknown you must be ready to stand for yourself. Colonists of Kagwa station quickly learned this lesson and now they willing to teach anyone brave enough to help at colonie defence. ..."

Are you ready to some physical activies? This area consists of Paintball playground and Lazertag areas that will keep you blood flowing. Recomended for the teenagers or adults.


Kagwa Station Colony

"Not all heroes wear capes. This saying is true even in furthest reaches of the space. Pilots and Soldiers collect all the glory, but the true backbone of the humanity are simple people: workers, sientists and artisans. Come and feel what does it feel to make a living on another planet..."

This is a recreational area that highly dedicated to the roleplay. Touch the atmosphere of the exoplanet colony and participate in the life of the different classes of the colony. Recomended for everyone.
